About Weather ex Machina

Different meteorological agencies often produce different weather forecasts for the same location and time. This leaves customers in an stressful predicament: which weather forecast can they trust, and how reliable is this prediction going to be. WeatherXM aims to reduce the uncertainty in weather forecasts.

We have created a service that aggregates and compares third party forecasts with real-time weather conditions from weather stations, in order to measure forecasting accuracy per location and forecasting agency .

We have concluded that some forecasting agencies persistently perform better than others on specific locations and/or weather conditions and we believe information like this could yield in to a new innovative product, as a smartphone app. We will also develop more innovative weather services in the future, taking advantage of our own weather data accumulated and produced by our app using a number of techniques such as:

  • crowdsourcing weather observations
  • user generated forecasts
  • internal mobile phone sensors
  • additional external hardware and more
